Safe and Mine

Pico Labs presents Safe and Mine, a Manifold application which helps you to manage your things.

How it works

  1. Affix a tag to your thing.
  2. Prepare a message explaining how to contact you.
  3. Lose your thing (hopefully not, but if you do...)
  4. When someone finds it and scans the tag, they see your prepared message.
  5. You arrange with the finder to return your thing.
